Olivia Bennett2024-08-11
Did the rafting (1/2 day Brown's Canyon) and ziplining package Overall a great experience - highly recommended.

Rafting Brown's Canyon - First off our river guide Sebastian was incredible. It was very clear that he was very experienced in this water and he guided us expertly through the Class II and Class III rapids while we shared a raft with a family of two with small children. Definitely a thrilling section of the river lots of white water but not crazy, so great for a adventurous family or group. The trip itself was a bit long 4 hours - about 25 minutes to check in and get ready up (helmet, PFD, optional life jacket and optional wetsuit provided), about 20 minutes to the river, about 2.5 hours down the river, then a 45 minute hike back. I would also like to give a round of applause to the tour leader Will for running everything smoothly while keeping things light and fun while talking safety/information.

After a lunch break (either bring lunch or if you have time go to Buena Vista for a bite), we headed off to the zipline adventure. Once again we had fantastic guides - Ernie and Torre. They brought a lot of positive energy and fun to the experience. In total, the ziplining took just under 2 hours from getting gear ready to the course and then a short hike uphill to the first platform. We spent just under 90 minutes on 6 different ziplines that offered amazing views while zooming along the mountainside. You can take your phone (just keep it in a zipped pocket or backpack) for great photos.

I think it would be remiss if I didn't mention that it's worth it to have a cancellation guarantee. The weather is very changeable We changed our package date twice to work with our other plans. The office staff was super helpful and I agreed when I called to adjust plans.

Overall, we had an incredibly fun day with Emeraft. In addition to having amazing experiences, we were very pleased with the high quality guides on each of our trips. And the price of our package (and their prices at the outpost for gear/clothing) was surprisingly reasonable.
