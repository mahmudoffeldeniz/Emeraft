How a package works

  • First Activity

A. First Activity

Embark on an unforgettable full-day adventure and experience the best of Colorado with two different activities. From one of our Idaho Springs or Buena Vista outposts, you will ascend to our Via Ferrata course. Your group will spend the morning (or afternoon) traversing the mountainside on this unique assisted climbing experience. Utilize a combination of iron rungs, bars, and bridges to climb up and across the mountain, and even experience an exciting freefall. Our Via Ferrata courses are seamlessly integrated into the cliffside terrain, offering panoramic views of the Rocky Mountains and views of the Arkansas River Valley or Idaho Springs below. Your knowledgeable guide will provide comprehensive instructions on the course before leading you into your adventure.

View Packages

B. Meal

Following your initial activity, it’s time to recharge with a lunch break. Depending on your excursion, lunch arrangements vary: you will need to bring your own packed lunch, or opt to grab a bite at a local restaurant. Refer to your trip description for specific details. Your guide will keep you informed about the schedule and meeting point for your subsequent adventure. Certain trips offer an express option, enabling guests to forgo the lunch break and proceed directly to their next adventure experience.

View Packages

C. Second Activity

Following lunch, reconvene with your guide to prepare for the next thrilling chapter of your Colorado outdoor experience. Whether you’re embarking on a family-friendly excursion or tackling challenging class IV or V whitewater, your group will set out on one of Colorado’s premier rivers. Guided by our seasoned guides, you’ll spend the afternoon immersed in the region’s awe-inspiring landscapes, navigating rapids along the river’s course. Feel the refreshing spray against your skin and the warmth of the sun on your hair as you ride the currents, embracing one of Colorado’s most distinctive adventure offerings.

We offer rafting and Via Ferrata packages at both our Buena Vista and Idaho Springs outposts.

*The order of activities may vary depending on your trip. Please check your booking for exact details.

What to know about Raft + Via Ferrata Adventure

Best of Colorado

Wherever your Colorado adventure leads, you’ll be immersed in the splendor of the state’s great outdoors. Our Buena Vista outpost is fully immersed in the Rocky Mountains, and from here, paddle the best of the Arkansas River, keeping your eyes peeled for wildlife. Our Granite Via Ferrata course takes you high above the Arkansas River Valley offering vistas of the majestic Collegiate Peaks Mountain Range.

Alternatively, just 30 minutes from Denver, Idaho Springs boasts an adventure getaway from the city. Raft down Clear Creek, passing through the historic mining valley and the charming town of Idaho Springs, flanked by the towering Colorado Rockies. Our Mount Blue Sky Via Ferrata course at this location ascends into the cliffside for unparalleled panoramas of the town and Clear Creek below.

Options for Everyone

Offering a range of options from family-friendly rafting to advanced whitewater excursions, both the Arkansas River and Clear Creek stand out as premier rafting destinations in the state. Thrill-seekers can opt for packages featuring rafting on challenging class IV or V rapids, while families can delight in milder rafting experiences on class III rapids. Via Ferrata adventures at both outposts provide another exhilarating experience for those 12 and up. Via Ferrata courses do not require previous climbing experience yet offer an exciting and accessible adventure for everyone with a moderate level of physical fitness.

More Bang for Your Buck

A rafting and Via Ferrata package not only ensures that your group experiences a full day of outdoor fun but also optimizes your savings. We are committed to adventure opportunities that get everyone outside in Colorado’s great outdoors, with our multi-adventure packages intended to be a more cost-effective way to experience multiple trips. These packages provide the convenience of enjoying two adventures at a single location, along with experienced guides to ensure a seamless and fun day!

Two Convenient Locations

Choose a rafting and Via Ferrata package at our Buena Vista outpost for an authentic immersion into the heart of Colorado’s Rocky Mountains. Experience the thrill of rafting down the legendary Arkansas River, navigating through the stunning Browns Canyon National Monument, or one of the steepest sections of whitewater in the state. Your journey on our Granite Via Ferrata takes you up into the mountainside, offering unparalleled views of the Arkansas River Valley.

Meanwhile, for those seeking a convenient day trip from Denver, our rafting and Via Ferrata adventure packages at our Idaho Springs outpost promise equally memorable experiences. Delight in a rafting excursion along Clear Creek, with picturesque vistas of historic Idaho Springs, before taking flight on our Mount Blue Sky Via Ferrata, climbing into the foothills of the Rocky Mountains.

WHAT TO BRING

Your Via Ferrata + Rafting package is a full-day of physical activity and adventure. We strongly recommend guests bring a water bottle that can be clipped in to their harness for the Via Ferrata. Closed-toe shoes are required for the Via Ferrata. We also recommend guests wear long active pants or capris.

For rafting, guests must wear secure shoes (no flip flops or crocs), such as Tevas, Chacos, Keens or tennis shoes you don’t mind getting wet. Guests should also wear quick-dry clothing or a swimsuit for under their wetsuit. Please bring a change of clothes for the Via Ferrata tour. You will also need to either bring a lunch or grab lunch in town, depending on your trip location.

FAQs

With our rafting and Via Ferrata packages, you can seamlessly partake in both thrilling adventures within a single day, exploring Colorado’s rivers and mountains. A multi-adventure package is much more economical than booking two separate trips, while also guaranteeing a well-coordinated, excitement-filled day!

Upon booking your selected trip, please note that the order of adventures will be displayed on the booking engine. This order is determined by factors such as location, trip timing, and other considerations, and cannot be altered. No matter the order of your two adventures, we guarantee your group will have a remarkable experience!

Both our Buena Vista and Idaho Springs outposts feature unique rafting and Via Ferrata multi-adventure packages. Whether you hope to delve deeper into the mountains or stay closer to Denver, your adventure will be nothing short of extraordinary!

Please prepare for the rafting segment of your adventure by bringing suitable footwear. We recommend water shoes or other secure shoes that will stay on your feet and can get wet – no flip-flops or crocs, please! Additionally, we recommend quick-drying attire, sunscreen, and sunglasses secured with a strap. As for your Via Ferrata excursion, closed-toed shoes are mandatory, and we kindly request that you choose longer shorts or pants that can fit in your harness. Please avoid loose clothing, necklaces, or scarves. Depending on your trip location, you will need to either pack a lunch or grab lunch in town between adventures.
